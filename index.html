About me

Hello! My name is Yuhe Liang, and my nickname is Wallace. Currently, I work as a server tester in the server manufacturing industry. One of my weekly duties is to inspect and interact with the Performance Impact Assessment (PIA) Test, which is run on the servers at the end of their production process. I am also responsible for the maintenance and repair of the servers. Recently, I have been learning web development through a UC Berkeley Extension program, and this webpage is a showcase of the knowledge I have gained from the program so far. In my free time, I enjoy music and ice skating. I usually play my flute and piano at home on weekends, and I occasionally sing in the shower. Moreover, Ice skating has become my new favorite activity since 2022. I love the speed and smooth movement on the ice, which makes me feel like I am flying! This is me!! Part of me!!!
